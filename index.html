About the Artist

Emily Tschirhart grew up in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Throughout her early career, Emily was a vocalist, performing jazz, classical, and pop selections. As time went on, she began to write her own music as well. As a vocalist, Emily was a 2016 Songbook Academy finalist, a record-breaking finalist for Future Stars in Ann Arbor, and singer for award-winning band, Rosewood.

In addition to personal accolades, Emily's band Rosewood won Ann Arbor Battle of the Bands two years in a row, headlined festivals Live On Washington and Top of the Park, and performed on the radio, on many occasions.

As a solo artist, Emily was awarded the Philo T Farnsworth Award for Best Student Original Music Video, participated in University of Michigan's Girls In Music and Technology Program, and was chosen to particpate in the CalArts Capitol Records Jazz CD.
